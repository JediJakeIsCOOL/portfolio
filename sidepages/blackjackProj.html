Project Summary

This BlackJack application was created using HTML, CSS, and Object oriented JavaScript. Being a new developer and trying to get some of the methods and objects to work together was quite a challenge but yielded good experience. The game works as classic blackjack does. The cards are dealt and then the player is given an option to hit or stand. If the player "stands", the dealers sequence of cards are dealt out and the dealer gets cards until they are at 17 or higher in points. Having higher points than the dealer when the dealer is done getting cards or the dealer has busted, results in a victory for the player. Likewise if the players points exceed 21 points then the player busts and it's the dealers victory. Any results in the application leading to the dealer or players victory removes all button usage except "play again".
